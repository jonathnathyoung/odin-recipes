Lasagna

Description

This is a special lasagna recipe - the result looks like tranya from Star Trek's Corbomite Maneuver but when consumed it tastes exactly like a classic Italian lasagna.

Ingredients

  • 12 tranya berries
  • 3 ripe tomatoes
  • ½ pound ground beef
  • 1 pound shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 package lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions
  2. Peel tranya berries
  3. Put tomatoes in plastic bag and smash against the counter
  4. Brown ground beef in saute pan, then add tranya berries and tomato
  5. Place one layer of lasagna noodles in a lasagna or similarly sized pan
  6. Layer the filling and spread mozzarella cheese on top, then repeat until pan is filled
  7. Finish with one last layer of cheese
  8. Bake for 15 minutes in a 450°F oven
